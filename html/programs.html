Programs

Our Programs

Ghana Education Service

We follow the Ghana Education Service curriculum,
ensuring high academic standards and holistic
development. Students are prepared for excellence
with a strong foundation in knowledge,
values, and skills.

British Curriculum

We offer a world-class education
which follows the British Curriculum with
a focus on critical thinking and
creativity. Our approach prepares students for
global opportunities and academic excellence.

Montesorri System

We follow the Montessori approach,
nurturing independence, curiosity, and a love
for learning. We provide a child-centered
environment that encourages exploration and
holistic development.

Our Curricular Activites

Reading Camp

Our Reading Camp fosters a passion for books
and literacy. Through fun activities and guided
reading sessions, we help students improve their
reading skills and imagination.

After School Camp

Our After-School Camp provides a safe and
engaging space for students to explore hobbies,
learn new skills, and socialize. We nurture
creativity and growth beyond the classroom.

Swimming

Our swimming program teaches essential water
safety and swimming techniques. We help
students build confidence, fitness, and a
lifelong love for swimming.
